Reaching Out

To reach out, please fill out the required information using the contact form below. We will be happy to answer any queries relating to our mission in raising awareness of the United Nations effort to end poverty in all its forms everywhere. Your inquiries could range from how we measure impact, to our collaboration with local and international NGOs, or even ways you can get involved.

Please use the form to ask about volunteer opportunities, partnership possibilities, or to learn more about specific projects we're currently undertaking. Together, we can make a significant difference, so do not hesitate to reach out with your questions or ideas.

Contacting Us

When sending an inquiry or feedback, please include your full name, email address, and a clear description of your request or the information you need. This helps us respond more efficiently and effectively.

Can I give feedback?

Absolutely! We value your input and are always looking to improve our content and user experience. Please use the contact form to submit any suggestions or comments you have about the website or our articles.

Response Times

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. If your matter is urgent, please indicate this in your message, and we will do our best to prioritize your request.
