Overview

We believe that if we all collectively come together and take action, we can positively contribute towards Sustainable Development Goals such as eliminating poverty and inequality across the world.

Importance of UNDP

The United Nations Development Programme (UNDP), helps countries develop policies, leadership skills, partnerships and institutional capabilities to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals.
